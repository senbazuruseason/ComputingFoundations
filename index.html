Experiments in Computer Science

I grew up alongside the internet and came to love computers at an early age. I created my first website using Angelfire when I was a 13 to publicly call out a boy that had broken my friend's heart. I watched as my older brother took apart computers in his spare time, and I tagged along with him to LAN parties at the computer shop that his friend owned. Surfing the web and interacting with technology came pretty naturally to me. So, while computers have always been a part of my life, I did not completely dive in to my interest until the creation of the Google DeepDream Project became open source. After a steep learning curve and many hours spent metaphorically banging my head against a wall in order to make things work, I realized that this sort of challenge was something that I loved. Since then, I have been investigating and playing with technology in various ways.
